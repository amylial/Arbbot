What it Does

The basis of our idea starts with Arbitrage. Investopedia defines Arbitrage as the simultaneous purchase and sale of an asset to profit from an imbalance in the price. Arbitrage exists as a result of market inefficiencies. As you can see from the simple graph above, there is a difference between the ask price of exchange A, and the bid price of exchange B. We buy from exchange A and then sell on exchange B to generate a net profit.

Arbitrage For Dummies

In simple terms, the bot buys low and sells high. Say market A was trading apples at 1$/kg, and market B was trading apples at 2$/kg. The bot sees this and buys apples from market A, and sells it on market B.
