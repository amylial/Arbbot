Our Services

What Our Bot Can Do For You

For a fee, we will help trade cryptocurrencies for you using our bot. You can choose which cryptocurrencies you would like to trade, and out bot will automatically trade them for you when favourable to generate a profit. You can choose when you want to stop trading, and withdraw your investment.

Fees

The fee will depend on a variety of things, such as how much you're trading, and which cryptocurrencies you want to trade. You can choose to trade as much or as little as you want. In general, we will charge a percent of your investment, and the rate will change depending on how much you invest.
